# YouTube Music App v1.0.0 Patch Notes

We're excited to release version 1.0.0 of the YouTube Music App! This update brings a range of improvements and features to enhance your experience.

## New Features and Enhancements

- **Initial Release:** This marks the first official release of the YouTube Music App! Enjoy the convenience of accessing YouTube Music directly from your desktop.
- **Sleek User Interface:** Experience a minimalistic and sleek user interface designed for easy navigation.
- **Quick Access:** Open the YouTube Music website within the app and enjoy a seamless browsing experience.
- **Optimized for Windows 11:** The app has been tested on Windows 11 to ensure compatibility and performance.

## Important Notes

- **Loading Time:** Please note that the app may take a few moments to load, especially the first time it's launched. This brief delay is due to the WebView content loading process.
- **Feedback:** Your feedback is important to us! If you encounter any issues or have suggestions for improvements, please don't hesitate to reach out to us.

## Known Issues

- None reported at this time. We're committed to addressing any issues that may arise and providing timely updates.

## How to Get Started

1. Install Dependencies: Make sure you have the required dependencies by following the instructions in the README.
2. Run the App: Run the `music.py` script to launch the YouTube Music App.
3. Access YouTube Music: Click on "File" in the menu bar and select "Open YouTube Music" to start exploring YouTube Music within the app.
